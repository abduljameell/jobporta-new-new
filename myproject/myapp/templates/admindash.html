About Us

Welcome to the Job Portal Admin Dashboard. This platform helps administrators manage job seekers, recruiters, and job postings efficiently.

Our mission is to provide a seamless connection between job seekers and employers, ensuring a smooth hiring process.

Admins are responsible for verifying recruiters, monitoring job listings, and maintaining platform integrity.
